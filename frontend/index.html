Daily Health Tips for Wellness

Simple, actionable wellness guidelines to maintain daily body health.

Stay Hydrated

Aim for 2.5 to 3 liters of water daily. Hydration keeps mucous membranes moist, flushes out pathogens, and maintains blood volume.

Prioritize Sleep

Get 7 to 9 hours of quality sleep. During sleep, your immune system releases cytokines, which are essential to fight infections.

Anti-Inflammatory Diet

Eat fresh fruits, ginger, garlic, and turmeric. Avoid high-sugar, oily foods that elevate systemic inflammation levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions regarding symptoms predictions and safety.

Is MediPredict AI a substitute for real doctors?

No. MediPredict AI is an educational assistant designed to help patients understand symptom patterns. It is not a clinical diagnosis. Always consult a certified healthcare professional for medical diagnoses and treatment plans.

How accurate is the symptom prediction?

Our machine learning classifier is trained on symptom-disease patterns. While highly accurate in analyzing data matching the symptom profiles, it is intended for educational guidance only.

Is my health data secure?

Yes. Your diagnostic histories are stored securely in a dedicated MongoDB Atlas cluster and are only accessible through your private practitioner/user token credentials.
